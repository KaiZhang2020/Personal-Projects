Our Story

Lijie LLC is founded in 2016, as the first Chinese-American owned cleaning, landscaping, and construction company in the Seattle area, we are proud to serve our community with quality services and a commitment to excellence. Our core values include integrity, reliability, and attention to detail. We strive to build long-lasting relationships with our clients and provide personalized services that meet their unique needs.

Our mission

We strive to enhance the beauty and functionality of our clients' properties through our expertise in cleaning, landscaping, and construction. We believe in using eco-friendly and sustainable practices to create a healthier and more beautiful environment for our clients and the community. We are dedicated to exceeding our clients' expectations and providing them with the highest level of satisfaction.
